About Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was the 11th President of India and is widely referred to as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology. Beyond science, he was known for his humble lifestyle, love for teaching, and deep connection with youth and students across India.
